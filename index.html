Dan Han

PhD Candidate in Policy Analysis

Pardee RAND Graduate School; RAND Corporation

I am a PhD Candidate in Policy Analysis at the Pardee RAND Graduate School, and an Assistant Policy Researcher at the RAND Corporation. My area of specialization is health policy. I work on studies in both US and international contexts, and often use insights from economics to guide my research.

My research interest lies in health care payment policy and the economic behavior of hospitals. Recently I have also become interested in behavioral factors that underlie physician decision making.
